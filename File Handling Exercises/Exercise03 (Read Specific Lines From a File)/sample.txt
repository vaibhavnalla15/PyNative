1.	“Vasundhara Kutumbakam” means the whole world is one big family.
2.	It teaches us to treat every human being with love and respect, beyond religion, nation, or race.
3.	This principle is India’s gift to the world — promoting unity, peace, and global harmony.
4.	As an Indian value, it reminds us to serve not just our people, but all of humanity.
5.	It inspires the idea of global brotherhood, compassion, and oneness.
